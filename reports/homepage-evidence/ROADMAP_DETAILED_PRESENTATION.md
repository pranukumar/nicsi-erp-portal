# NICSI ERP Portal - Detailed Roadmap (Presentation Format)

## Slide 1: Title
- NICSI ERP Portal
- Strategic Roadmap for International-Level Digital Governance Platform

## Slide 2: Why Transformation
- Move beyond a standalone website/ERP model.
- Build a common digital governance platform for multi-stakeholder operations.
- Improve transparency, trust, and measurable service outcomes.

## Slide 3: Transformation Principles
- Security by design
- Interoperability by default
- Data-driven governance
- Modular and reusable architecture
- Inclusive and accessible user experience

## Slide 4: Capability Layer Model
- Experience Layer: citizen, department, vendor interfaces
- Workflow Layer: configurable process orchestration
- Data Layer: master data, quality, reporting, analytics
- Integration Layer: APIs, events, adapters
- Trust Layer: identity, audit, compliance, security
- Operations Layer: reliability, observability, DR

## Slide 5: Governance and Operating Model
- Governance council for policy and platform decisions
- Defined owners: business, product, data, security, operations
- Standard change control, release control, and compliance reviews

## Slide 6: Core Workflow Modernization
- End-to-end file and approval lifecycle digitization
- Configurable workflows for department-specific needs
- SLA dashboards with automated escalations

## Slide 7: Procurement and Vendor Modernization
- Tender lifecycle transparency checkpoints
- Vendor onboarding and empanelment workflows
- Compliance and performance scorecards

## Slide 8: Identity, Security, and Trust
- Unified SSO with MFA
- RBAC + ABAC access model
- Centralized security logs and monitoring
- Tamper-evident audit and legal traceability

## Slide 9: Data Governance and Analytics
- Master data ownership and governance
- Data quality validation and reconciliation
- Executive dashboards and decision intelligence

## Slide 10: Interoperability and Ecosystem Integration
- API-first service exposure
- Event-driven inter-system communication
- Standardized contracts for cross-platform compatibility

## Slide 11: Citizen and Stakeholder Experience
- Unified interaction journeys
- Multilingual and accessibility-compliant interfaces
- Faster support and self-service design

## Slide 12: Reliability and Continuity
- High availability architecture
- Disaster recovery patterns and readiness drills
- End-to-end observability and operational runbooks

## Slide 13: Capability Building and Adoption
- Role-based training programs
- Department onboarding playbooks
- Adoption metrics and continuous improvement loop

## Slide 14: Innovation Pipeline
- AI-assisted policy analytics and document intelligence
- Controlled pilots for emerging governance technologies
- Innovation backlog linked to measurable outcomes

## Slide 15: Executive Ask
- Approve platform transformation direction
- Constitute governance and ownership structure
- Prioritize cross-department onboarding
- Formalize security and interoperability standards
