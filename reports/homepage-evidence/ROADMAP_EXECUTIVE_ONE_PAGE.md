# NICSI ERP Portal - Executive One-Page Roadmap

## Vision
Transform NICSI ERP Portal into an integrated Digital Governance Platform for departments, citizens, and vendor ecosystem.

## Strategic Outcomes
- End-to-end transparent governance workflows
- Faster, audit-ready decision making
- Secure and interoperable digital public infrastructure
- Scalable service delivery across organizations

## Priority Tracks
1. Governance and Platform Operating Model
- Platform governance council, ownership matrix, release controls.

2. Core Workflow Digitization
- Configurable approvals, file movement, procurement, SLA and escalation.

3. Unified Identity and Access
- SSO, MFA, role/attribute-based access, privileged access governance.

4. Procurement and Vendor Ecosystem
- Tender lifecycle modernization, empanelment, compliance tracking.

5. Data Governance and Intelligence
- Master data, quality controls, executive dashboards, policy analytics.

6. Interoperability Layer
- API gateway, event bus, standardized integrations with ecosystem systems.

7. Security, Compliance, and Trust
- Zero-trust posture, SOC/SIEM integration, tamper-evident audit trail.

8. Citizen and Stakeholder Experience
- Unified service touchpoints, multilingual support, accessibility-first UX.

9. Reliability and Continuity
- High availability, DR readiness, observability and SRE practices.

10. Capability Building and Adoption
- Training academy, onboarding kits, adoption scorecards.

## Platform Positioning Statement
NICSI ERP Portal is evolving into a secure, interoperable, and analytics-driven Digital Governance Platform that enables accountable and high-scale public service operations.
